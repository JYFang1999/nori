About This Experience

Step into a tranquil studio in the heart of Hongdae and learn the ancient art of pottery. Whether you're a complete beginner or have dabbled with clay before, our experienced instructors will guide you every step of the way as you create your very own handmade soju cup — a personal keepsake you'll treasure forever.

Designed for solo explorers, friends, and couples, this 2-hour session is the perfect way to slow down, get your hands dirty, and leave with something truly handmade.

What You'll Do

1
Step 1
Meet your instructor
Arrive at the studio, get settled, and receive a warm welcome from your pottery guide.
2
Step 2
Learn pottery basics
Discover clay techniques, how the wheel works, and what makes a great soju cup.
3
Step 3
Shape your clay
Hands on the wheel — feel the clay come alive as you form your unique piece.
4
Step 4
Personalize your design
Add textures, patterns, or carvings to make your cup unmistakably yours.
5
Step 5
Glazing and finishing
Choose from a range of glazes to give your piece its final colour and sheen.
6
Step 6
Collect your final piece
Your creation will be fired and ready to collect within 5–7 days — or shipped to you.
